Guidelines for Recruiters in the URUG Slack community:

1) Identify yourself as a recruiter and identify the agency/company you represent.
2) Please keep job posts in the appropriate channel. 
3) All job posts must include all of the following:
    - Location of position(or remote if applicable)
    - Name of company
    - Type of position (Full Time/Part Time/Internship, W2/1099/Contract to hire etc...)
